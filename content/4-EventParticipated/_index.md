During my internship in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program, I had the opportunity to participate in two technical meetup sessions organized by the program. These events provided valuable opportunities to explore emerging technologies, learn from experienced engineers in the fields of Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Artificial Intelligence (AI), and broaden my understanding of modern software development practices and career pathways.

By attending these meetups, I was able to stay up to date with current technology trends while gaining practical insights from industry professionals. The knowledge and experiences I acquired significantly supported the development of my internship project and strengthened my foundation for pursuing a career in Cloud Computing.

## Meetup 1

- **Event Name:** *Docker, Cloud Computing, and Modern Software Architecture*
- **Date:** June 6, 2026
- **Location:** First Cloud AI Journey Office
- **Role:** Participant

The first meetup brought together speakers specializing in Cloud Computing, DevOps, and AI, covering a wide range of topics including Docker, Domain-Driven Design (DDD), Event-Driven Architecture, GraphRAG with Amazon Bedrock and Amazon Neptune, integrating AWS WebSockets with Godot, and a Machine Learning-based Network Intrusion Detection System (NIDS) on AWS.

Among these sessions, the presentation on **Docker and Containerization** was particularly impressive. It helped me understand the differences between Virtual Machines and Containers, Docker's architecture and workflow, how to build Docker images using Dockerfiles, optimize images through layer caching, and apply Docker effectively in software development and deployment.

Through this meetup, I gained a deeper understanding of modern software architecture, including Event-Driven Architecture, Domain-Driven Design, and the benefits of Containerization for improving application deployment, scalability, and management on cloud platforms.

## Meetup 2

- **Event Name:** *DevOps Career Orientation and the Cloud Technology Ecosystem*
- **Date:** June 13, 2026
- **Location:** First Cloud AI Journey Office
- **Role:** Participant

The second meetup focused on career development in DevOps and Cloud Computing. The speakers shared valuable insights into the real-world responsibilities of DevOps Engineers, recommended learning paths for beginners, practical experiences working in technology companies, and the implementation of a URL shortening service on AWS.

One of the most valuable sessions was delivered by **Trong Hoang Truong**, who explained the daily responsibilities of a DevOps Engineer and emphasized the importance of mastering fundamental knowledge such as Linux, Networking, Git, Docker, CI/CD, and Infrastructure as Code (IaC). He also highlighted the value of systems thinking, automation, and understanding the root cause of problems rather than relying solely on tools.

This meetup provided me with a clearer understanding of the DevOps career path and demonstrated how modern cloud technologies and automation tools contribute throughout the software development lifecycle.

## Conclusion

The two meetup sessions organized by the First Cloud AI Journey program provided me with valuable knowledge and practical experiences beyond my daily internship tasks. In addition to learning about modern technologies such as Docker, Domain-Driven Design, Event-Driven Architecture, and DevOps, I also had the opportunity to hear real-world experiences from engineers working in the industry regarding project development, problem-solving, and career growth.

The knowledge and experience gained from these events have strengthened my understanding of modern system design, cloud-based software development, and professional best practices. More importantly, they have helped me establish a clear learning roadmap and prepare for future AWS certifications while pursuing a long-term career in Cloud Computing.
